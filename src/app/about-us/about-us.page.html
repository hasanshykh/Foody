Feedy

This app allow its user to share and receive surplus food in real time in order to help fight against food wastage and hunger issues. Considering this issue, we brought up with a solution that refers to services that bring together the concept of food sharing/receiving in a city with the use of technology, where every user can share/donate surplus food from anywhere (home, restaurants, marriage halls, parties, etc.) and the other user who seeks food for himself or to help some needy (white collars, people in slum areas or anyone who canâ€™t afford food) can pick the food from anywhere navigated with our integrated Google Maps navigation system.
